Our Mission


Everyone has had the experience of going to a clinic, or emergency room, only to wait for hours upon hours without resolution. At Health Connect, we understand this frustration and aim to reduce the stress and hesitancy caused by healthcare by providing everyone with an accessible and intuitive platform with information on your nearby hospitals and clinics!




Why Health Connect?


Health Connect combines the several services on one platform to provide users with reliable information regarding hospitals and clinics! Our platform generates real-time updates on the wait times of emergency rooms and clinics and calculates the best healthcare location to travel to.
